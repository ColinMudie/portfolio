Who am I?

My name is Colin Mudie and I recieved my undergraduate degree from Central Michigan University in Music Performance. While music is still such a large portion of my life I have decided to look towards my other passion to create a career in. I started a coding bootcamp through MSU in Nov, 2020 and have found new realizations. My original drive was to be able to afford more modular synthesizers (Those aren't cheap!) but I am now also driven by the thrill of learning. I love the idea of always having someting to learn and with this field, while I have learned so much already, I can understand that I will never truly know everything about Fullstack Web Development. Thats awesome! I'm always going to have goals moving, never stagnate, and will always be learning everyday!
